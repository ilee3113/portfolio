About Me

About Me

Peter Lee was born and raised in the Atlanta area and is a proud ATLien. He studied engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology and business at Georgia State University then proceeded to work as as a consultant in a fast paced, client driven Big 4 firm. With a passion for data, He pursued a career pivot to become a data scientist. Outside of work, He enjoys food, sports, and board games. In his freetime, Peter enjoys day trips with his girlfriend.
